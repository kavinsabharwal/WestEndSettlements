Privacy Policy

At West End Settlements, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website and settlement services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details (email address, phone number), property details, and other information relevant to your settlement transaction. This information is collected when you request a quote, contact us, or engage our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and manage your property or business settlement.
  • Communicate with you regarding your transaction.
  • Respond to your inquiries and quote requests.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may disclose your information to:

  • Government bodies (such as Landgate and the Office of State Revenue) as required for your settlement.
  • Financial institutions involved in your transaction.
  • Other parties strictly necessary to complete your settlement (e.g., real estate agents, other settlement agents).

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
