About Us

Honesty, Courtesy, and Respect Since 1995.

West End Settlements began as a settlement agent in Perth in 1995 (formerly West Coast Settlements). Since amalgamating with "Property People Settlements" in 2001, we have grown into one of Perth's most respected independent agencies.

We pride ourselves on receiving the majority of our clientele through referrals. This is a testament to our friendly, professional attitude and genuine helpful assistance.
